# 🌟 Brio

**Skills training app for teens (ages 13-21)**

Learn academic and life skills at your own pace through interactive, visual, and engaging lessons.

---

## 🎯 Purpose

Brio helps teens develop practical skills for college and independent living through:
- **Montessori-style learning** - Learn by doing, not reading
- **Minimal text** - Icons and visual cues
- **Self-paced** - No pressure, progress at your speed
- **Gamification** - Streaks, XP, levels, and badges

## 🎨 Design Philosophy

### **1. Teen-Friendly, Not Childish**
- Modern, clean interface
- No cartoon graphics or elementary visuals
- Respectful tone

### **2. Minimal Words**
- 1-3 words per card maximum
- Icons over text
- Interaction over explanation

### **3. No Therapy Language**
- Avoid clinical terms
- Natural, casual language
- Normal app feel

### **4. Learning by Doing (Montessori Style)**
- See → Try → Feedback
- Hands-on interaction
- No lecture-style instruction

### **5. Visual First**
- Big icons (48px+)
- Color cues
- Drag-and-drop interactions
